Research

My research interests are mainly:

  • coalescent processes (such as Kingman’s coalescent, multiplicative coalescent)
  • random graphs (such as Erdös-Rényi and inhomogeneous random graphs)
  • stochastic models for biology (most of them linked to population genetics)
